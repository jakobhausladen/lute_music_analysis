I spin, when God gives me the means

                                                                                                 -3-
I spin my distaff on my way.
I entered a garden,
(I spin when God gives me the means)
I found three flowers there,
I go, I come, I turn, I twist, I bind,
I trim, I clip, I shave.
I dance, I leap, I laugh, I sing, I ready myself.
I guard my sheep from the wolf.
I spin my distaff on my way,
I spin, when God gives me the means,
I spin my distaff on my way.
