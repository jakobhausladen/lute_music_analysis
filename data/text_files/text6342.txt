 "leaden strings" is low and slow

 "and doth in highest" is in the high register

 "as any challenged echo clear" echos the abovementioned passage

 ""But when she doth of mourning speak" is mournful

 "her sighs" has a rest to emulate a sigh, or possibly to allow the singer actually to sigh

 in the lute part on "strings do break", it sounds as though the strings are breaking
