Sweet memory of consummated pleasure
O happy time, which causes such knowledge!
The steadfastness of the two of us, so in love,
that was so strong in overcoming all our ills,
now has lost its power,
Breaking the end of my only hope.
The good is finished; suddenly the bad begins.

The good in finished; suddenly the bad begins.
O happy heart, that can nonchalantly deal with
the cruelty, malice, and inconstancy
that one often sees in the feminine will.
He who scorns it has no regrets,
for virtue will cause his reputation to grow,
leaving her being such a loyal duty.
Sweet memory of consummated pleasure.
